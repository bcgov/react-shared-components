# Footer

Footers help people find what they need after scrolling to the bottom of a web page.
They provide supplementary information such as copyright, contact
information, social media links, and links to other pages within a website.

## When to Use

This footer must appear on all public-facing online B.C. Government content and services

## When not to Use

When the product or service is not owned by the Government of British Columbia

## API

No explicit properties are required to be passed in while consuming this component.

<br />

Used at the bottom of the page.
